Robotics Workshop

Advancements in technology are happening every single day. Students around the world are being exposed to the advantages of learning robotics and understanding their place in society today. It is also an important pathway for career opportunities in the future. Therefore learning robotics is an added skill that will go a long way. This workshop is a two day Robotics Development program. The primary focus of this workshop is to enable the students to build and program robots on their own from scratch.

The two day program is streamlined in such a way that beginners with no prior programming experience will be taught how to build and program their own robots by the end of the workshop.


Concepts covered:

  • Basics of embedded systems

  • Introduction to AVR Series Micro-Controllers & ATmega 16 micro-controller.

  • An overview of open source hardware/software and resources required to kick start a robotics project

  • Software overview and introduction to embedded C programming

  • Explanation of LED, LCD, ADC, IR Sensors, ultrasonic sensor, DC Motor, motor driver and their interfacing

  • Robot chassis building

  • Obstacle avoiding robot

  • Line following robot


The Take-away kit includes:

  • DVD containing all software, datasheets and resources required for the student for future assistance

  • ATmega 16 development board by Grape Labs (1)

  • USBasp Programmer (powered by ATmega 8) by Grape Labs (1)

  • Data cable (1)

  • LCD (1)

  • LCD connector (1)

  • IR transmitter and receiver (1 pair)

  • DC geared motor (2)

  • Burg wires (10)

  • 9V cell (1)

  • Battery connector (1)

  • Mechanical components required to build a robot.
